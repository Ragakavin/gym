Body Type Guide & Visual Reference

Ectomorph

Lean, Fast Metabolism

• Naturally lean with small joints and muscles
• High metabolism - burns calories quickly
• Difficulty gaining muscle
• Strategy: Higher calorie intake, focus on strength training

Mesomorph

Athletic, Balanced

• Athletic build with good muscle definition
• Moderate metabolism - gains/loses weight easily
• Naturally muscular frame
• Strategy: Balanced diet with moderate calories, mix of cardio and strength

Endomorph

Stocky, Slow Metabolism

• Rounder body shape with slower metabolism
• Gains weight easily, harder to lose
• Naturally stronger in lower body
• Strategy: Lower calorie intake, more cardio, protein-rich diet
